---
title: Standard vocabularies considered during the analysis phase
---

During the analysis phase of some metrics, such as the authors of a KG, it is necessary to consider the predicates from some vocabularies. The following is a list of the vocabularies that were considered (which are also indicated in [this paper](https://bit.ly/3R8tYBA)).

- [SKOS](https://www.w3.org/TR/swbp-skos-core-spec/)
- [RDF Schema](https://www.w3.org/TR/rdf-schema/)
- [SKOSXL](https://www.w3.org/TR/skos-reference/skos-xl.html)
- [DublinCore (DC)](https://www.dublincore.org/specifications/dublin-core/dcmi-terms/)
- [FOAF](http://xmlns.com/foaf/spec/)
- [POWDER-S Vocabulary](https://www.w3.org/2007/05/powder-s)
- [schema.org](https://schema.org)
- [DCAT](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-dcat-2/)
- [XHTML](https://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml/vocab)
- [VoID](https://www.w3.org/TR/void/)
- [OWL](https://www.w3.org/TR/owl-guide/)
- [SWP](http://www.w3.org/2004/03/trix/swp-1)
